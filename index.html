What is 2020?
inspired by CNN's own year in review.

2020 has been one rollercoaster of a year.



All of us have been undoubtedly affected. There is not a single person on this entire Earth that isn't part of this historical period. We have been bombarded with natural disasters of all sorts, such as the bushfires in Australia, the locusts in Africa, and COVID-19.
Although the year is, unfortunately, not yet over, there are already have enough significant events to create a memoir for a decade, let alone a single year. We have compiled all the historical events of this year into an informative timeline - so get ready to take a trip down memory lane (or rather, trauma lane).
