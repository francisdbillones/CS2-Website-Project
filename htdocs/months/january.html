Australian bushfires

Fires rapidly spread across all states to become some of the most devastating on record. An area about the size of South Korea, roughly 25.5 million acres, has burned. Around 3,000 homes have been destroyed or damaged.

Taal Volcano eruption

The Taal volcano erupted this January 12 on the Philippine island of Luzon, spewing ash up to 9 miles in the air and forcing large-scale evacuations. The eruption has left behind a desolate landscape of destroyed houses and snapped trees covered in a thick layer of heavy ash.
source: CNN
image source: Rappler

Plane crash causes tension between US and Iraq

When the United States killed Iranian commander Qassem Soleimani on January 3, it also killed Abu Mahdi al-Muhandis, a powerful Iraqi militia leader. The move critically destabilized U.S.-Iraq relations. It also sparked WW3 memes on Twitter.
