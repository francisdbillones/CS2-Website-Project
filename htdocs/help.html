Want to help?

Donating is one of the most generous and noble acts anyone can do. If you are lucky enough to not experience the woes of 2020, feel free to help those that did.

UNICEF

By donating to UNICEF you are directly supporting malnourished children, children at high risk of disease, and children with no access to education.

Donate to: UNICEF

Red Cross

By donating to Red Cross you are directly supporting medical professionals that provide care for those that can't afford it. You are also helping them distribute food and essentials.

Donate to: Red Cross

Beirut

The Beirut explosion was one that dominated headlines at the beginning of August. A threatening mushroom cloud hung over the city. Donate to Preemptive Love, a charity providing relief and care for Beirut's citizens.

Donate to: Preemptive Love

UNHCR

By donating to UNHCR, you are directly supporting victims of 2020's worst disasters, such as the Taal volcano eruption, the California fires, the Australian wildfires, even the Beirut explosion.

Donate to: UNHCR

WHO

By donating to WHO you are helping prevent the spread of COVID-19 and stopping it from defacing our world further.

Donate to: WHO

DonatePPE

By donating PPE to frontliners, you are helping them prevent the spread of COVID-19 and helping provide PPE for countries that do not have access or the resources.

Donate to: DonatePPE
